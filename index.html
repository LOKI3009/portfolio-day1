PCA

PCA (Principal Component Analysis) in DWDM is a statistical method used to simplify large monitoring data. In DWDM systems, many parameters like power, OSNR, and BER are measured for multiple channels. This creates complex and high-dimensional data. PCA reduces this data into a few important components. It helps in detecting faults and analyzing system performance easily. Thus, PCA improves monitoring and optimization of DWDM networks.
